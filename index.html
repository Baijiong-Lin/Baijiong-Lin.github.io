Biography

I'm Baijiong Lin, a research assistant from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), working closely with Dr. Yu Zhang. Before coming to SUSTech, I received my Bachelor degree in Information and Computing Science in 2020 from School of Mathematical Sciences at South China Normal University (SCNU), where I focused on Gravitational Wave Data Analysis Based on Deep Learning and advised by Prof. Xiangru Li.

My research interests include but are not limited to Few-Shot Learning, Meta Learning, Transfer Learning and Computer Vision. And I currently focus on Cross-Domain Few-Shot Learning.
