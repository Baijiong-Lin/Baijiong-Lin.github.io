Biography

I'm Baijiong Lin, a research intern in SmartMore Corporation Limited. Before that, I had a great time at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), working as a research assistant in Median Group led by Prof. Yu Zhang. I received my Bachelor degree from School of Mathematical Sciences, South China Normal University (SCNU) in Jun. 2020.

I am interested in the foundation of machine learning including multi-task learning, meta learning, and transfer learning and their industrial applications.
